Know Your Policy!


Third Party

Third-party insurance offers protection against damages to the third-party by the insured vehicle. It covers physical injuries, damages to the vehicle, damage to the property, and death. Exclusions. Third-party insurance does not provide any compensation, if: The accident was caused due to drunken driving.


Comprehensive

comprehensive car insurance is a combination of third party insurance and own damage insurance. This car policy provides comprehensive coverage against third party liabilities and loss or damages caused to the insured car due to accidents, vandalism, fire, theft, manmade and natural calamities.
